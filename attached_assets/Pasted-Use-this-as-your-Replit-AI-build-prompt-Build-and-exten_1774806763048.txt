## Pricing Logic

Implement the following pricing rules exactly as the business logic for the estimator.

### Minimums and Limits

* 4 hour minimum on all moves
* Maximum working time is 10 hours
* Travel time is included in hours worked
* Example: if the move is 6 working hours and 2 hours of travel, quote 8 hours total

### Standard Crew / Hourly Pricing

#### One bedroom plus one living room

* 2 movers
* $165 per hour
* estimated 5 hours
* fully furnished assumption

#### Two bedroom plus one living room

* 3 movers
* $200 per hour
* estimated 6 hours
* estimated around $3,000 in fuller jobs, but use the hourly formula for base estimator and allow a higher-range message if inventory is heavy

#### Three bedrooms plus one living room

* 3 movers
* $200 per hour
* estimated 8 hours
* estimated around $3,000

#### Four bedrooms plus one living room

* 4 movers
* $300 per hour
* estimated 8 hours
* estimated around $4,000

### Garage / Outdoor Rule

If there is a garage or outdoor furniture, add:

* +1 extra mover

### Materials Pricing

Charge materials per bedroom as needed:

* Stretch wrap: 1–2 per bedroom at $55 each
* Tape: 1 roll per bedroom at $13.50 each
* Small box: $3.50 each
* Medium box: $6.50 each

### Deposit Rules

* If total quote is under $1,000, required deposit is $50
* If total quote is over $1,000, required deposit is 50% of total
